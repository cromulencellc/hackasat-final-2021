# Management Center Credentials

Username and password for the management center are set in the "docker-compose.yml" file.

## Default Credentials 
Username: cedalo 
Password: mmcisawesome.

Update this entry in the "docker-compose.yml" file to set your credentials:

CEDALO_MC_USERNAME: cedalo
CEDALO_MC_PASSWORD: mmcisawesome

Restart the Management Center for the changes to take effect.
